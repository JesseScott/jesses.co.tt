CineCitta



CineCitta is my VJ persona, having performed at clubs, festivals, and special events all around the world, with both fully articulated AV sets, and stricly visual sets for guest musical artists. The name harkens to the origins of my VJ practice gestating at the same time that I dropped out of film school, and my interest in burgeoning forms of experimental cinema... The work is primarily undertaken with custom software, though in my early days it tended to be more sample-based.
