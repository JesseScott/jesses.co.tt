Patients First



Patient's First was an iPad application that I prototyped as part of a mentorship for 4th Year IxD students at Emily Carr University of Art + Design, for the Health Design Lab, in a project led by Jonathan Aitken.

The iPad app is designed to be a tool to empower and encourage medical patient to become more engaged in his/her healthcare. The goal is to lend some transparency to the hospital stay, with the patient’s scheduled appointments and visit clearly displayed in the calendar, while capturing the emotional aspects of being a patient. The app functions as a hub for all the patient’s appointments and visits, and provides a clear picture of the care team.

My role involved assisiting in the user experience flow, technical vetting of the design, and programming the app in Xcode.

The project page can be seen at Fraser Health Project and the source code can be found on my GitHub.
