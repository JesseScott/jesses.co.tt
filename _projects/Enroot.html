Enroot



Enroot is an iOS & Android application that I am involved in devloping for Enroot Meals Ltd. I have been primarily responsible for the Android application, but have consulted heavily on the iOS client, as well as having been partially responsible for the backend and UI/UX design.

The Enroot service is a delivery-anywhere commissary kitchen, where your smartphone acts as a menu and payment service, allowing you to quckly order food to your exact GPS location. As part of this, I had to handle Geolocation, user management, database access, cryptography, and financial transactions in the development.

The application is currently undergoing Beta testing, and will be available on the App Store's soon.

Source code is not available, as this is a client project.
