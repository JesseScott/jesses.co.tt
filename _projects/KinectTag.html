Kinect Tag



Kinect Tag. An early prototype of a kinect-based graffiti tool. Peep the Wu.

Using the OSCeleteon library for Processing to track skeletons, and the GML4U library for saving to Graffiti Markup Language.
Made at Onformative.
Slightly different flavour than Jean-Christophe Naour's.
