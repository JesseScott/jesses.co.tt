Point Cloud Street View



Point Cloud Street View was a speed project - a quick test of attaching MS Kinect and Laptop to a DC Inverter with a 12V deep cycle battery, all on top of a cargo trike.

Using SimpleOpenNI wrapper for Processing, I simply biked around Kreuzberg, Berlin and saved the frames...
